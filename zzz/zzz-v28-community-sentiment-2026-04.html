1. Executive Summary

Overall Sentiment: 🟡 Mixed (Promeia) + 🟢 Positive (Starlight Billy)

TikTok's top priority-window post — @gazedbythevoracity, 38.3K likes, “I'm so happy yall have no idea” — is a direct response to a design fix. The controversy narrative is shifting from “problem” to “they listened.”

4. YouTube Indonesia

Sentiment: 🟢 Positive | 5 videos, 66 comments

Kit discussion dominates Promeia comments. Indonesian creators are excited about the Ice Anomaly kit and Abloom synergy, but the conversation has shifted toward budget calculation — “how many pulls do I need” and “F2P math” are the dominant comment types. Pull intent is real but financially constrained.

The KamarRama drip video (Kamen Rider crossover theory) drew 40 comments alone, confirming the Kamen Rider/tokusatsu hook is a genuine engagement driver for Starlight Billy in Indonesia. This is culturally specific — Kamen Rider has a strong Indonesian fanbase.

Comment sentiment on YouTube is more positive than Facebook or X. YouTube viewers arrive via algorithmic recommendation and tend to be pre-sold on the content.

11. Conclusion & Data Notes

Conclusion

v2.8 is a banner of two halves. Starlight Billy is the cleanest re-engagement opportunity ZZZ has had — universally liked, culturally resonant in Indonesia (Kamen Rider), and driving account reactivations across every platform monitored. Promeia enters with real but fragile pull intent: dedicated savers are confirmed, the design controversy is being actively resolved by Hoyoverse, and the community is shifting toward a “they listened” narrative.

The Indonesian market is distinct: Billy's appeal is amplified by tokusatsu culture, Promeia's criticism is modesty-framed rather than aesthetic, and Facebook is the dominant platform for mass-market sentiment. Global channels (Reddit, X) show sharper polarization but should not be conflated with Indonesian sentiment.

⚠️
Key risk: NTE (Apr 29) and WuWa v3.3 (Apr 30) launch in the same window as v2.8 (May 6). Attention and wallet competition will be fierce.
✨
Key opportunity: Billy's “King Billy” narrative and Kamen Rider hook are underutilized in official and KOL content. There is room to amplify an already-organic story.
